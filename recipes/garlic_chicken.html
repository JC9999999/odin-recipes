Garlic chicken

Description:

Garlic friend chicken is a tender, juicy chicken. The pieces are coated in a flavorful garlic based batter or breading. The chicken is fried until it is golden brown and crispy. The chicken is often marinated in a mixture of garlic, soy sauce and other seasonings for enhanced flavorful

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 cup all purpose flower
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed

    • Steps:

    1. In a shallow dish, mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread crumbs and flour. In a separate dish, whisk together the milk and egg.
    2. In a shallow dish, mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread crumbs and flour. In a separate dish, whisk together the milk and egg.
    3. Fry chicken in the hot oil for about 5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Remove from the oil with a slotted spatula, and serve.
